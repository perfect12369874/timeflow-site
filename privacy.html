隐私政策Privacy Policy

TimeFlow（「我们」「本 App」）由独立开发者维护。本政策说明 TimeFlow 如何收集、使用、存储和共享数据。 TimeFlow ("we", "the app") is maintained by an independent developer. This policy explains how TimeFlow collects, uses, stores and shares data.

最后更新：2026 年 6 月 25 日Last updated: June 25, 2026

简而言之In short

  • ✓你的时间记录数据存在你的设备，并通过你自己的 iCloud 私有库（端对端加密）在你的设备间自动同步。Your time data lives on your device and syncs across your devices via your own private iCloud (end-to-end encrypted).
  • ✓我们没有服务器，没有账户系统（无需注册登录），不收集分析数据，也读不到你的数据。We have no servers, no accounts (no sign-up), collect no analytics, and cannot read your data.
  • ✓只有你主动触发 AI 洞察时，部分数据才发给你自己配置的 AI 服务商。Data is sent to your own configured AI provider only when you actively trigger an AI insight.
  • ✓你随时可以选择上传给 AI 的数据范围（L1 / L2 / L3）。You choose what's shared with AI at any time (L1 / L2 / L3).

The app reads or creates the following on your device; except those marked "Never leaves device", they live on your device and sync via your own private iCloud:

2数据如何离开你的设备How data leaves your device

你的数据会自动同步到你自己的 iCloud（端对端加密，见第 4 节）—— 只在「你的设备 ↔ 你的 iCloud」之间流转，苹果和我们都读不到。除此之外，数据离开你的掌控只在你主动触发下面这些情况：Your data auto-syncs to your own iCloud (end-to-end encrypted, see §4) — flowing only between your devices and your iCloud, unreadable by Apple or us. Beyond that, data leaves your control only when you actively trigger the following:

当你点「生成 AI 洞察」时，TimeFlow 会向你自己配置 API Key 的 AI 服务商发起一次 HTTPS 请求。payload 取决于你在「设置 → AI 隐私级别」选择的等级：When you tap "Generate AI insight", TimeFlow makes one HTTPS request to the AI provider you configured with your own key. The payload depends on the level set in Settings → AI privacy level:

L1隐私优先Privacy-first

只发送聚合统计：各分类时长占比、每日睡眠总分钟、最长连续天数等。不发送项目名、地点、原始时间块。Sends only aggregate stats: category share, daily sleep minutes, longest streaks, etc. Never project names, locations or raw blocks.

L2匿名上下文Anonymized context

在 L1 基础上 + 匿名化时间块图案（起止、分类、时长）。项目名替换为 Project A / B / C。不发送项目原名、地点、备注。L1 plus anonymized block patterns (start/end, category, duration). Names become Project A / B / C. No original project names, locations or notes.

L3完整上下文Full context

在 L2 基础上 + 项目名、自定义分类名、运动细节。只有 L3 会包含可能识别你身份的内容。隐私级别可在「设置 → AI 隐私级别」随时切换。L2 plus project names, custom category names, motion detail. Only L3 may include identifying content. Switch levels anytime in Settings → AI privacy level.

AI 服务商如何处理你的数据How AI providers handle your data

每家服务商的隐私政策由其各自管理。TimeFlow 与任何一家都无商业关系或数据共享协议。建议你在使用前阅读所选服务商的政策：Each provider's privacy policy is its own. TimeFlow has no commercial relationship or data-sharing agreement with any of them. We recommend reading your chosen provider's policy first:

  • Anthropic (Claude) — anthropic.com/legal/privacy
  • Google (Gemini) — policies.google.com/privacy
  • DeepSeek — platform.deepseek.com/privacy
  • Moonshot (Kimi) — moonshot.cn/privacy
  • 字节跳动 · 火山引擎（豆包）ByteDance · Volcengine (Doubao) — volcengine.com

语音补记（可选）Voice capture (optional)

若你在「设置」里开启「豆包流式识别」并用语音补记时间块，录音会实时上传字节跳动 / 火山引擎做转写；不开启则使用 Apple 设备端识别，完全不联网。这条数据绝不发给任何 AI 洞察服务商或其他第三方。If you enable "Doubao streaming recognition" in Settings and dictate a block, the audio is streamed to ByteDance / Volcengine for transcription; otherwise Apple on-device recognition is used and nothing goes online. This audio is never sent to any AI-insight provider or other third party.

3你的 API KeyYour API key

  • API Key 由你自带（从各服务商控制台获取）You bring your own key from the provider's console
  • 存储在 iOS Keychain，属性为 WhenUnlockedThisDeviceOnlyStored in iOS Keychain as WhenUnlockedThisDeviceOnly
  • 不会通过 iCloud 同步到其他设备Not synced to other devices via iCloud
  • 删除 App 即清除 Keychain 中所有 TimeFlow 项Deleting the app clears all TimeFlow items from Keychain

4iCloud 同步（自动）iCloud sync (automatic)

登录 iCloud 后自动开启：Enabled automatically once you're signed into iCloud:

  • 时间块、项目、分类、AI 洞察、自定义地点（含坐标）通过 NSPersistentCloudKitContainer 同步到你私有的 iCloudBlocks, projects, categories, AI insights and custom places (incl. coordinates) sync to your private iCloud via NSPersistentCloudKitContainer
  • 数据仅在你自己的设备之间同步，由 CloudKit 端对端加密，Apple 和开发者都读不到Data syncs only between your own devices, end-to-end encrypted by CloudKit; neither Apple nor the developer can read it
  • API Key 不会被 iCloud 同步，需在新设备重新填入The API key is never synced — re-enter it on a new device
  • 未登录 iCloud 时，数据只留在本机，一切照常Without iCloud, data simply stays on your device — everything still works

5数据保留与删除Retention & deletion

  • 数据在设备上保留，直到你主动删除或卸载 AppData stays on device until you delete it or uninstall
  • 「设置 → 数据与隐私」可一键导出（JSON / CSV）或清空Settings → Data & privacy lets you export (JSON / CSV) or wipe in one tap
  • AI 服务商保留你请求数据的时长由各家政策决定，TimeFlow 无法控制How long AI providers retain your request data is governed by their policies, beyond TimeFlow's control
