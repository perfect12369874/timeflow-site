01 · 入门Getting started

我需要每天手动开始 / 停止计时吗？Do I have to start / stop a timer every day?

不需要。开启 健康 + 运动 + 位置 权限后，TimeFlow 会被动识别睡眠、通勤、移动状态，自动补全你的时间表，你只要确认。需要精确追踪某件事时，从桌面小组件点一下分类就能主动计时——也不用打开 App。

No. With Health + Motion + Location permissions on, TimeFlow passively detects sleep, commutes and movement and fills in your schedule — you just confirm. When you want to track something precisely, tap a category from the home-screen widget to time it actively — no app launch needed.

我没有 AI 服务商账号也能用吗？Can I use it without an AI account?

能。AI 洞察是可选功能。即使不连 AI，TimeFlow 也能完整记录、统计、显示趋势与目标进度。

Yes. AI insight is optional. Without any AI connected, TimeFlow still records, tallies, and shows trends and goal progress in full.

支持哪些 AI？哪个更划算？Which AI providers are supported? Which is best value?

你自带 Key（BYOK），可在 设置 → AI 提供商 里随时切换：

  • Claude（Anthropic）— 中文 + 长上下文分析最稳
  • Gemini（Google）— 免费额度大，速度快
  • DeepSeek — 价格最低，推理强
  • Kimi（Moonshot）— 中文 + 国内访问友好
  • 豆包（方舟）— 国内稳定、延迟低

You bring your own key (BYOK) and can switch anytime in Settings → AI provider:

  • Claude (Anthropic) — most reliable for Chinese & long context
  • Gemini (Google) — generous free tier, fast
  • DeepSeek — lowest cost, strong reasoning
  • Kimi (Moonshot) — great for Chinese & China access
  • Doubao (Volcengine) — stable & low-latency in China

02 · 数据 & 隐私Data & privacy

我的数据存在哪里？Where is my data stored?

默认全部在你 iPhone 本地的 SwiftData 数据库里。如果开启 iCloud 同步，数据通过你私有的 iCloud 在你的 Apple 设备间同步。我们没有服务器，看不到你的数据。详见 隐私政策。

By default, everything lives in a local SwiftData database on your iPhone. If you enable iCloud sync, data syncs across your Apple devices through your private iCloud. We have no servers and cannot see your data. See the Privacy Policy.

发给 AI 的数据是什么？What data is sent to the AI?

取决于你的 AI 隐私级别：

L1只发聚合占比 / 时长统计

L2+ 匿名化时间块图案（无项目名 / 无位置）

L3+ 完整上下文（项目名、自定义分类名）

随时在 设置 → AI 隐私级别 调整。

It depends on your AI privacy level:

L1aggregate share & durations only

L2+ anonymized block patterns (no names / no location)

L3+ full context (project & custom category names)

Adjust anytime in Settings → AI privacy level.

怎么删掉所有数据？能导出吗？How do I wipe everything? Can I export?

删除：设置 → 数据与隐私 → 清空所有数据。直接卸载 App 也会一起清除 Keychain 里的 API Key。

导出：设置 → 导出 / 备份 → JSON 或 CSV，通过分享面板发送或存到 iCloud Drive。

Wipe: Settings → Data & privacy → Erase all data. Deleting the app also clears the API key from Keychain.

Export: Settings → Export / backup → JSON or CSV, via the share sheet or to iCloud Drive.

03 · 功能Features

分类和项目有什么区别？What's the difference between categories and projects?

分类是顶层，固定 7 个（睡觉 / 工作 / 生活 / 健身 / 学习 / 反思 / 娱乐），不能改名或删除，但能改 emoji / 颜色。项目是分类下你自己创建的子项——比如「工作」下可以有「Project Alpha」「SysOps Migration」。

Categories are the fixed top level — 7 of them (Sleep / Work / Life / Fitness / Study / Reflect / Fun); you can't rename or delete them, but you can change emoji / color. Projects are sub-items you create under a category — e.g. "Project Alpha" and "SysOps Migration" under Work.

能设置目标吗？Can I set goals?

每个项目都能。编辑项目 → 打开「设定目标」→ 选周期（每天 / 每周 / 每月 / 每年 / 间隔 N 天）+ 时长。在 洞察 → 目标 里能看到所有进度，每周复盘会推送给你。

Yes, per project. Edit project → enable "Set goal" → choose a cycle (daily / weekly / monthly / yearly / every N days) + duration. See all progress in Insights → Goals, with a weekly review pushed to you.

怎么修改已记录的时间块？How do I edit a recorded time block?

点一下时间块就会弹出编辑底卡，可以调起止时间、时长、分类、项目，也能补一句语音或文字备注。日历上直接拖拽也行，15 分钟精度。

Tap a block to open the editor sheet — adjust start/end, duration, category, project, and add a voice or text note. You can also drag it on the calendar; 15-minute precision.

为什么我的睡眠图表是空的？Why is my sleep chart empty?

说明 健康 app 没给到睡眠数据。两个可能：① 没有睡眠追踪——在 健康 app 手动添加，或开启 iPhone / Apple Watch 自动睡眠检测；② 没给 TimeFlow 健康权限——设置 → TimeFlow → 健康 → 允许「睡眠分析」。

It means the Health app has no sleep data. Two causes: ① no sleep tracking — add it manually in Health, or enable auto sleep detection on iPhone / Apple Watch; ② TimeFlow lacks Health permission — Settings → TimeFlow → Health → allow "Sleep Analysis".
